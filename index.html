About us

SPME(Summer Programs Made Easy) was made by Kurren Singh, Mahak Kumawat, Jayden Castro, and Mesbahur Sakib.
Our goal is to make the search for summer programs easy, fast, and effective while being free and easily accessible.
We have done this by creating a website that has all you need through an database.
Our main goal/mission is to provide a wide range of programs, so no matter the criteria, you will find a program for you.
We hope you find this program to be easy and effective.
